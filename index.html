Fleet

Record the voyage number and select your faction.

See #criminal-enterprise for your fleet's current voyage number—for example, “143rd”.

Ship

Choose a ship name, and the record the class of ship sailed on the voyage.

See #captain-ships-of-the-cabal for your fleet's ships.

Job

Record the job type.

Sloop voyages are always Errands.
You must be rank Smuggler to post an Errand.
You must be rank Boastwain to post a Heist.
See #how-to-make-money for details.

Crew

Record your names and rank first, then the rest of the crew. If sailing with additional crew, record a participation percentage for each (up to 10 crewmates).

Use gamertags so Discord autocompletes them—for example, “OldGus” autocompletes for “OldGusClone”.
See #ranks-and-promotion for more.

Bonus

Check if the crew is eligible for bonus.

Type Prerequisites Bonus
Each crewmate has a different specialty rank. $1,000
Each crewmate belongs to one of any two factions. $2,000
Each crewmate belongs to a different faction. $2,500
All crew belong to the same faction. Each has a different specialty rank. $2,500
Additional bonuses are available on a brigantine or galleon.
Purchase a specialty rank from #pirate-emporium for $10,000. Specialty ranks are tagged on Discord names.
Dice, Pai Gow, Blackjack, and Craps Table apply automatically:
Type Prerequisites Bonus
Dice Pays for each Stowaway or Recruit. $1,000 (Errand); $500 (Heist)
Pai Gow All crew are rank Doubloon Shark or lower. $3,000
Blackjack Total plunder is $170,000–$210,000. $3,000
Craps Table Total plunder is $620,000 or higher. $5,000

Activities

Record up to 25 activities, and how many times you completed each.

You can create your own activity log later if you prefer.

Plunder

Record your gold and doubloon amounts at the start and end of the voyage.

Save in-game screenshots of your starting and ending gold and doubloons, in the event you need to verify your voyage.

Summary

Be brief, but evocative. What was the goal, or vibe? Did anything interesting happen?
