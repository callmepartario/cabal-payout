Bonus

Check if the crew is eligible for bonus:

Type Prerequisites Bonus
Each crewmate has a different specialty rank. $1,000
The crew represents exactly two Cabal of Thieves factions. $2,000
Each crewmate is a member of the same Cabal of Thieves faction, and each has a different specialty rank. $2,500
Each crewmate represents a different Cabal of Thieves faction. $2,500
Additional bonuses are available when sailing a brigantine or galleon.
Visit the #pirate-emporium to purchase a specialty rank ($10,000). Specialty ranks display as an icon on your Discord name in chat. A Royal Flush bonus supersedes a Straight Ship bonus.
These bonuses are applied automatically:
  • Dice: Stowaway or Recruit aboard ($1,000 each on an Errand, $500 each on a Heist)
  • Pai Gow: While on a brigantine or galleon, all crewmates are rank Doubloon Shark or below ($3,000)
  • Blackjack: Total plunder is $170,000–$210,000 ($3,000)
  • Craps Table: Total plunder is $620,000 or more ($5,000)
