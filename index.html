Bonus

Available bonuses depend on ship, job type, and crew makeup.

These bonuses are applied automatically:
  • Pai Gow: Sail a brigantine or galleon with no officers aboard ($3,000)
  • Blackjack: Total plunder is $170,000–$210,000 ($3,000)
  • Craps Table: Total plunder is $620,000 or more ($5,000)

Dice Bonus

For each novice aboard, Dice pays $1,000 on an Errand, or $500 on a Heist.
